CREDIT PROTECTION

Your newly acquired purchase will enhance everyday life, but what if things changed suddenly due to an accident or illness? It's still the responsibility of the owner to make the remaining loan payments. With credit protection you're protecting your good name, valuable credit rating and ensuring your family won't be burdened with the financial obligation should something happen.

  • Life Insurance Coverage

    The balance of the loan is completely paid off in the event of death due to sickness or injury.

  • Critical Illness Insurance Coverage

    The balance of the loan is completely paid off in the event the owner is diagnosed with a critical illness.

  • Disability Insurance Coverage (Accident & Sickness)

    Monthly payments are covered during time of need following an injury or sickness that prevents them from working.

  • Involuntary Unemployment Insurance Coverage

    The monthly payments are covered during time of need if the owner was to become involuntarily unemployed.

EXTENDED WARRANTY

Your new unit is an investment and to protect that investment, it should be in your best interest to have a protection plan put into place. The coverage is easy to understand and there won't be the surprise of deductibles or unexpected limitations.

  • Avoid Expensive Repair Bills

    The coverage is there whether a repair bill is a few dollars or a few thousand dollars. The cost of several years worth of coverage is just a fraction of what a single repair bill could cost.

  • Added Value For Your Investment

    The coverage is fully transferable, increasing the value of the unit in a future sale or trade.

  • Real Peace of Mind

    Benefits including roadside assistance, rental coverage and trip interruption are available as part of the extended warranty (on applicable units).

  • Guaranteed Price Refund Commitment

    If the customer goes claims free for the entire term of their contract, they may be entitled to get a refund (on applicable units).
